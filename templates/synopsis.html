For the past several decades, scientists have been monitoring
the mysterious depletion of the earth's water supply.
  Once thought to be a renewable resource, water is now increasingly scarce,
and no plausible factor has been identified as the cause.
Is it all a result of climate change?
Or perhaps something even more sinister.
Something...extraterrestrial?
The world is counting on you. Solve the problems in each
challenge and gather clues as to the mysterious
disappearance of earth's most precious resource.
Your first clue,
start at the worlds highest point
and see what new information can be uncovered there.
